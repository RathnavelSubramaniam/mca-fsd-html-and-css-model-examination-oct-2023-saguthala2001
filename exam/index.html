About RVS COLLEGE:

RVS College of Arts and Science (RVS CAS) is an ISO 9001:2000 certified institute in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is established in the year 1986.

The college is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. The campus is located adjacent to the Coimbatore-Trichy highway at Sulur.
